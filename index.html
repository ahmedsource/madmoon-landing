Welcome to Madmoun, your go-to digital escrow app for secure and seamless transactions. Whether you're buying or selling online, Madmoun ensures that your transactions are protected, giving you peace of mind throughout the process.

How Madmoun Works

1

The seller creates the transaction and sends a request to the buyer

Start by creating a transaction on Madmoun. Provide details about the goods or services involved, including the agreed-upon terms and conditions.

2

The buyer accepts that request

Madmoun acts as a neutral third party. We verify the details provided by both the buyer and the seller to ensure a clear understanding of the transaction.

3

Deposit Funds

The buyer deposits the agreed-upon funds into the Madmoun escrow account. This step shows the seller that the buyer is serious about the transaction.

4

Fulfillment

The seller then delivers the goods or services as agreed upon. Madmoun monitors this process to ensure that everything is in order.

5

Approval and Release

Once the buyer is satisfied with the delivery, they approve the transaction. Madmoun then releases the funds to the seller. If any issues arise, our support team is here to assist.
